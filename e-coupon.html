OVERVIEW

Partner E-coupon is a benefit that should let partners use it conveniently. Meanwhile, it should be share/send to friends or manage coupons easily. The current Partner E-coupon distribution is based on SMS and is not easy to use. So we consider using WeChat, a more extensive and efficient way to let our partners use and manage their coupons.

HOW MIGHT WE

Clearly understand
the status of each coupon

QUOTES:

I Don’t know the status of each coupon. I may forget that coupon has been used or sent out.

INSIGHTS:

Provide a way to tell partners about the status of the coupon after receiving a lot of coupons and using or sending to friends.

POTENTIAL SOLUTION:

Set and display
different status of tag on the coupon.

Put coupons into
a unified entrance to use

QUOTES:

No one uses SMS now. It’s so annoying that I have to find my coupons in SMS.

INSIGHTS:

WeChat “Enterprise Official Account” or “cards and offers” could be used as a common entrance.

Displaying coupons
more logical

QUOTES:

I need to scroll to the bottom to use the coupons of a few months ago, or may have expired.

INSIGHTS:

Use the coupons of previous months as usual, but could be aware of new received coupons every month.

POTENTIAL SOLUTION:

Having a switcher to sort coupons by month.

Allow to manipulate
multiple coupons at the same time

QUOTES:

Too many steps for sending coupons to friend, I need to repeat the same operation many times.

INSIGHTS:

User can send multi-coupons by one click. Avoid to do duplicate actions.

POTENTIAL SOLUTION:

Give an option to do multi-item selection and actions.

Information Structure
